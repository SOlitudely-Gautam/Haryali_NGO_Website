Who We Are

Hariyali is an action-oriented NGO dedicated to Environment Conservation, established in 1996 by CA. Prof. Punam Singavi. With branches at Thane, Mulund, and Dombivli, we carry out activities with participation from Corporate Offices, Colleges, School students, and green enthusiasts.

Hariyali undertakes green initiatives such as water conservation, rainwater harvesting, nirmalya collection, composting, sapling preparation, plantation drives, and eco-friendly awareness programs.

The organization also regularly conducts various activities for school and college students, inspiring them to connect with nature and take responsibility for the environment around them.

Ayurveda & the Medicinal Plants Portal

Ayurveda, an ancient Indian system of medicine, emphasizes the healing power of plants. Hariyali, in collaboration with IES Management & Research Institute, Bandra and Mulund College of Commerce, has developed this portal to spread awareness about the utility of medicinal plants.

This initiative promotes sustainable living and traditional knowledge. The information covers the medicinal plants repository maintained at Hariyali nurseries. For the benefit of the common person, all audio explanations have been developed in Marathi.
