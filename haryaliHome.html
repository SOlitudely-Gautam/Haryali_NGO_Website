NGO Hariyali was started in 1996. The main goal of Hariyali is to develop forest. It is also involved in various green initiatives like developing nursery, holding exhibitions and connecting communities with environment awareness to improve eco-friendly practices, i.e, composting, parasbaug. This is also another practice to share information as per ayurvedic science through exhibition. Medicinal plants information repository is developed under this website. The main feature is that the audio is developed in Marathi.
